### How Giga works ...

LIVE SUPPORT EXECUTION
Giga works like a real support rep
During live support, via chat or voice, Giga takes action directly inside your systems. It signs in as a support rep, navigates your internal tools, and completes requests end-to-end.

From request to execution
A self-serve way to get work done
Users initiate tasks directly from a widget inside your product. Giga navigates the interface and completes the workflow step by step.


### Browser Agent
A new kind of chat experience.
Giga doesn’t just respond. It acts directly inside your product to complete the task.

### The conversation becomes actionable
The customer explains what they want in natural language.
Giga understands the intent and determines the steps required to complete the task.

### Execute in a secure cloud browser
With user approval, Giga temporarily takes control of the cursor inside the website.
Every action is visible, and the user can stop the process at any time.

### Resolved and confirmed live
Giga navigates the interface as a human would.
It clicks, selects, validates inputs, and progresses through the full workflow to completion.
